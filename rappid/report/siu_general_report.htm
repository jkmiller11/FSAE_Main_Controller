Device Settings for: SIU

General



MCU of part number MPC5604B is loaded with package 144 QFP and MCU Major and Minor revision Number are 0x00 and 0x00

Digital Filter Prescalar is set to 1, hence resulting Digital Filter period is
Prescaled Filter Clock Period = T (IRC) x (IFCP + 1)
Where T (IRC) is one time period of internal oscillator
=62.5 X 10^-9*(0 + 1)
=62.5 X 10^-9 secs
